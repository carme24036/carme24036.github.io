Guess the number



This is a game where you have to guess a number within a certain range that progressively gets higher with each level. To play, click the first button below.
To keep playing the same level, just press enter on the keyboard when you guess the number.

You get a certain amount of points in each level, depending on its difficilty.
The more points you have, the more levels you can play. You can also gain points from playing the minigame. (Still in progress)

Also, for now, you can't exit the "game" until you guess the right number.

(This game is still a work in progress, this is just all that's been done at this time)
